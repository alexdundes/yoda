# ADR (Architecture Decision Records)

## What it is

A structured record of architectural decisions: context, options, decision, consequences.

## Why it inspires YODA

- Keeps technical memory and rationale.
- Avoids rework and repeated debates.
- Helps agents and humans understand the reason for constraints.

## How it appears in YODA

- Issues can reference relevant ADRs.
- Important decisions stay versioned and traceable.
