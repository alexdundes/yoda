# Visual identity and logo

## Chosen direction

- Simple icon that works as a favicon.
- Black and white (light background and dark background).
- Documentation symbol with the letter Y.

## Concept

- A square representing a document.
- A strong Y at the top as a title.
- Two or three lines below representing text.
- No fold, no shadows, no extra elements.

## Variations

- White background with black Y and lines.
- Black background with white Y and lines.

## Goal

Communicate: documentation + YAML/YODA + simplicity and clarity.
